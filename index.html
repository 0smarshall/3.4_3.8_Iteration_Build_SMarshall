What makes us special?

Here at Disconnected Vintage, we have one of the largest online ranges of vintage streetwear in New Zealand! Shop from over 900 unique vintage Sweatshirts, T-shirts, Jackets, Bottoms, and more! We also have an incredible range of vintage streetwear brands such as Nike, Carhartt, Tommy Hilfiger, Ralph Lauren, Harley Davidson, Adidas, Nautica, Guess, Calvin Klein, and more! Vintage, straight to your door in no time!
